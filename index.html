ABOUT COMPANY

We are a luxury nail salon operating out of Seattle, Washington. Founded in 2020, we aim to give clients the best experience possible. We offer services including manicures, pedicures, massages, and facials. We use only the highest quality products and our technitions go through a rigorous training process.

OUR SERVICES

REGULAR POLISH

Choose from a variety of colors of regular polish! Designs add to the total cost.

GEL MANICURE
